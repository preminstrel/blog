    Eclipse First, the Rest Nowhere.

    Overview

    Uma Musume Pretty Derby has two seasons, I prefer the later season, adaption based on true story of Tokai Teio (トウカイテイオー). The plot is ordinary, even you can preview the next race’s result. However, Cygames, the producer of Uma Musume anime, made it remarkably extraordinary through its expressive pictures. Comparing to season 1, from my point of view, season 2 takes a leap and noticeably wins more applause. The main character of it is Tokai Teio, different from main character of season 1, Special Week (スペシャルウィーク), Tokai Teio’s life is not really going smoothly. Four fractures created the Tokai Teio’s miracle.

    「母ちゃん化」makes Uma Musume a hit, ウマ娘 successfully presents characteristics of horse in the history and brings them to the same stage. To be frank, I am not interested in sports. However, Uma Musume makes me know that horse racing can be such enthusiastic. From the anime, unconsciously, I am exposed to knowledge related to horse racing, such as Triple Crown, 「天皇賞」,「有馬記念」. Every horse has its dream, the reason to race. The anime gives the horses personalities and dreams, which can be a significant factor to make it a success.

    The angle of horses

    Career

    One of flash point of Uma Musume is that it makes horses have the dreams and emotions like people. Thus, horses have the reason to race for themselves, not for trainers or audience. The most iconic role is Tokai Teio, my favorite character. Tokai Teio lost her chance to achieve Triple Crown and Undefeated one. Due to it, she was confused that why she run and the real meaning of running. She wanted to give up running and turn to support others. However, another misfortune happened. After the victory of the Takarazuka Memorial, Mejiro McQueen (メジロ マックイーン ) was unfortunately suffering from zonular ligament inflammation(悬韧带炎) on his left leg while preparing for the Autumn Emperor Award. Therefore, Tokai Teio decided to run again to encourage McQueen and she succeeded creating the miracle.

    Failure & fractures

    Comparing to Specil Week, Tokai Teio has the remarkable body ability and talent. Thus, at the beginning of the anime, Tokai Teio has won Double Crown in an invincible posture. However, due to her soft knees, she can boost easily than others while she can be vulnerable on another hand. Fracture ended Tokai Teio’s first dream. After fail to achieve her dream, Teio set up another dream: Undefeated and fail when competing with McQueen. Such failure and diseases depressed her, making her afraid of running and competing, just lowering her head and saying “I cannot”.

    Friends

    Tokai Teio lost her mind and confidence. The trainer made her do some behind-the-scenes work. When doing such works, Teio’s state of mood has changed slowly. When asking McQueen why she runs, McQueen smiled and told her that because of she was looking forward Teio’s revival. Teio’s confuse vanished quickly and devoted to train herself as before. Finally, she achieved a miracle as promised successfully.

    History based

    An interesting point is that every horse and race is based on the history authentically, including the results of races. Thus, Teio’s miracle is real history.

    Miracle

    It is an undoubtedly miracle! Episode 13 express Teio’s unbreakable will and stubborn confidence remarkably. I cannot express it very preciously, I regard that only when you watch such scene, will you feel the flow of emotions. Teio’s words and Narrator’s words can make me cry even just imagine the picture.

    Conclusion

    Let miracles become synonymous with will in that the authenticity of history proves it all.
